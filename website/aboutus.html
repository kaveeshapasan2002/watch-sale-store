Welcome To Timely Watches

A watch is a portable timepiece that has a movement driven either by spring or by electricity and is designed to be worn or carried in the pocket.

An unrivalled reputation for quality and expertise

Timely watch is an integrated and independent Swiss watch manufacture. Headquartered in Geneva, it is recognized for its expertise and the quality of its products.

Hans Wilsdorf, founder of the company, instilled a notion of perpetual excellence, leading to major watchmaking innovations, such as the Oyster, the first waterproof wristwatch, and the Perpetual rotor self-winding mechanism.

The brand designs, develops, and produces the majority of its watch components in-house. timely watch also supports the arts and culture, sport, exploration, and those who are devising solutions to preserve the planet.
